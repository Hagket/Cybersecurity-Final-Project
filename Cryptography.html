Cryptography


Near the middle of the year, we learned about the cryptography of text and messages. We went over the Caesar cipher as well as the Vigenère cipher, picking them apart to see how they were used and how they work. We also learned how we could decrypt a message using letter frequency analysis as well as brute force cracking showing the strength of the 2 ciphers. We also researched other ciphers and presented them to the class.

The Reason Why I Chose This Topic:


I picked this topic because I really liked decrypting secret messages and it was used often in Cyberstart mostly at the start of the program. Learning about the Enigma machine from WW2 was also very interesting to me. That stuck out as one of the topics throughout the year.
