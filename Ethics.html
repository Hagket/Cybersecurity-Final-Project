Hacking Ethics and Legality


Around Mid-October we learned about ethics, more specifically hacking ethics and legality. We learned about white, grey, and black hat hackers as well as the difference between the 3. We learned about penetration testing as well as vulnerability assessments and how the government uses these to create a stronger field of security for their programs and websites.

The Reason Why I Chose This Topic:


I chose this topic because I thought it was unique to this class and what we have learned throughout the year. Making sure that us students understood what was legal and what are the boundaries of legal and ethical hacking. This made it worthy of being used as one of the 5 topics on this website.
